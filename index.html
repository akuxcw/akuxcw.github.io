Current Openings

There are several openings in the areas of atomistic modeling, multiscale analysis, FRP-bonded concrete systems and meso-scale/nano-scale experimentation. Applications are invited for the PhD study, research assistant/associate (RA) and post-doctoral associate positions. Interested candidates with high self-motivation, strong background in solid mechanics and Linux operation system, and good interpersonal skills are encouraged to submit an application for the above positions. Please send your CV to denvid.lau(at)cityu(dot)edu(dot)hk if you are interested in joining the group.
